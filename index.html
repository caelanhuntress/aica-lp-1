The Programme: Structured Where It Matters. Flexible Where It Counts.

This six-session programme combines shared foundations with customisable depth, ensuring organisation-wide alignment while allowing you to invest where impact is highest.

Sessions 1–3: Foundations (100-Level)

Every participant completes the same core workshops:

101 – AI Foundations – Understand what AI is, how it works, and what it means for modern work.

102 – Applied AI Ideation – Identify high-value, realistic AI use cases across roles and teams.

103 – Prompting Essentials – Learn how to work effectively with AI systems day-to-day.

These sessions establish a shared language, baseline competence, and confidence across your organisation.

Sessions 4–6: Electives (200-Level)

After the foundations, your organisation selects three elective workshops aligned to your priorities.

Electives span areas such as:

  • Productivity, Automation, and Operational Efficiency
  • Knowledge Management and Organisational Intelligence
  • Communication, Marketing, and Sales Enablement
  • Strategic Thinking, Decision-Making, and Planning
  • Learning, Creativity, Multimedia, and AI Governance

This ensures the programme is relevant, role-specific, and outcome-driven — not generic AI training.
